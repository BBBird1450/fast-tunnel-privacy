Privacy Policy

What Fast Tunnel Does

Fast Tunnel is a VPN configuration discovery and management application for Android. It collects publicly available V2Ray/Xray proxy configurations from open-source repositories on GitHub, organizes them, and allows users to connect through them or copy them for use in other applications.

Data We Collect

We do NOT collect:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number)
  • Browsing history or web activity
  • Location data
  • Any data transmitted through the VPN tunnel

Stored locally on your device only:

  • Network profiles (WiFi SSID names, carrier names) — used to match configs to networks
  • Configuration performance data (ping times, success/failure counts)
  • Your preferences and settings

All data is stored exclusively on your device using local encrypted storage (MMKV). We have no servers and cannot access your data.

Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob — May display rewarded interstitial ads (up to 3 per day when you open the app). Google may collect your device's Advertising ID (AAID) for ad delivery and analytics. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub — Config sources are fetched from public repositories. No authentication or user data is sent.

Advertising

We may show rewarded interstitial ads when you open the app (up to 3 per calendar day). Ads never block Refresh, Connect, or other core features. If an ad fails to load or times out, you proceed immediately. Ads are not required to fetch configs or connect.

VPN Tunnel

When you connect, a local VPN tunnel is created on your device using the open-source Xray-core engine. Traffic is routed through the proxy server specified in the config. We do not intercept, log, inspect, or modify your traffic. Proxy servers are operated by third parties.

Children's Privacy

This app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes

We may update this policy. The date at the top of this page will be revised when changes are made.

Data Deletion

You can delete all locally stored data at any time:

  • Open Fast Tunnel → Settings → Clear all configs
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data from your device

We do not maintain accounts or server-side storage. No request to us is required.
